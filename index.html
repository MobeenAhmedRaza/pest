Pest Management Services

Integrated pest management is a flexible, dynamic strategy, which needs updating periodically as information is received from management practice results. IPM programs have proven a track record of significantly reducing the risks and related to pesticides, while improving quality, health & welfare of environment. There are different techniques applied for pest control: mechanical control, biological control, environmental control, chemical control, consultation and advice. This includes the installation of monitoring devices that is supposed to monitor the situation of pest control in the premise of the client. The focus of pest management is to take the condition of the pest under control. Integrated pest management can provide long-term protection against pests if done correctly and consistently. We have the expertise in inspection, extermination and eradication of general pests.
